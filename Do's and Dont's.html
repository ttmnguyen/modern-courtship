The Do's and Dont's of Dating

Do

  • Treat and love others the way you want others to treat and love you.
  • Be patient, open-minded, and tolerant in love.
  • Give 100% of your love.

Don't

  • Make assumptions or keep your thoughts and emotions bottled up.
  • Hesitate or regret.
  • Lose hope or give up on love.
